History

AZFFI Builders is a Mysore-based construction company committed to superior quality, sustainability and innovation. We specialize in residential and commercial construction, architectural design, interiors and turnkey projects.

2016

Company Founded

AZFFI Builders was established and began operations as a PWD contractor with skilled engineers and laborers.

2018

Service Expansion

Expanded into architectural design, project management and full construction solutions.

2020

Turnkey Solutions

Introduced turnkey projects including interiors, kitchens, wardrobes, painting, cladding and electrification works.

2024

Trusted Builder

Recognized as a trusted builder in Mysore known for integrity, professionalism and timely delivery.
